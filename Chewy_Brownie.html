Chewy Brownies

Makes 16 Brownies

Total Time: 55 minutes, plus 45 minutes cooling time

Prepare Ingredients

  • vegetable oil spray

  • 1 ounce unsweetened chocolate

  • 3 tablespoons Hershey cocoa powder

  • 1/3 cup (2 2/3 ounces) water

  • 5 tablespoons vegetable oil

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted and cooled

  • 1 1/4 cups sugar

  • 1 large egg plus 1 large egg yolk

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • 3/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour

  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Gather Baking Equipment

  • Aluminum foil

  • 8-inch square metal baking pan

  • Ruler

  • Large zipper-lock plastic bag

  • Rolling pin

  • Large bowl

  • Liquid measuring cup

  • Oven mitts

  • Whisk

  • Rubber spatula

  • Toothpick

  • Cooling rack

  • Cutting board

  • Chefs knife

Instructions

  1. Adjust oven rack to lowest position and heat oven to 350 degrees. Make aluminum foil sling for 8-inch square metal baking pan. Spray foil with vegetable oil spray.

  2. Place chocolate in large zipper-lock plastic bag and seal, removing as much air as possible from the bag. Use rolling pin to gently pound chocolate into small pieces.

  3. In a large bowl, combine cocoa powder and pounded chocolate.

  4. Heat water in liquid measuring cup in microwave until hot, 1 to 1 1/2 minutes. Use oven mitts to remove measuring cup from microwave. Pour hot water over cocoa mixture and carefully whisk until chocolate is melted smooth.

  5. Whisk in oil and melted butter. Whisk sugar, egg and egg yolk, and vanilla until smooth. Add flour, chocolate chips, and salt and use rubber spatula to stir until combined and no dry flour is visible.

  6. Use rubber to scrape batter into foil-lined baking pan and smooth top.

  7. Place baking pan in oven. Bake until toothpick inserted in center comes out with few moist crumbs attached, 25 to 28 minutes.

  8. Use oven mitts to remove baking pan from oven. Place baking pan on cooling rack and let brownies cool completely in pan, about 45 minutes

  9. Use foil to carefully lift brownies out of baking pan and place on cutting board. Cut into squares and serve.
